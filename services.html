What We Offer

EB2-NIW (National Interest Waiver)

Professionals with advanced degrees or exceptional abilities can apply for an NIW if their work benefits the U.S. economy, health, or technology sectors. This pathway waives the job offer requirement, streamlining the process for high-impact applicants.

  • Advanced degree holders
  • Evidence of significant contributions
  • Impact analyses and recommendation letters
Learn More
Employment Visas

We offer comprehensive support across a range of temporary work visas, ensuring your application is complete, accurate, and submitted on time.

  • H-1B: Skilled professionals in specialty occupations
  • L-1: Intra-company transferees and managers
  • O-1: Individuals with extraordinary ability in arts, sciences, or business
Learn More
Adjustment of Status (Green Card Applications)

If you’re in the U.S. and meet eligibility criteria, we guide you through the adjustment of status process to obtain permanent residency without leaving the country.

  • Family-based petitions
  • Document preparation and evidence review
  • USCIS filing and follow-up
Learn More
Deportation

Facing removal proceedings? Our experienced attorneys provide strategic defense at each stage, from initial hearings to appeals, protecting your rights and future.

  • Master calendar and removal hearings
  • Responses to Notices to Appear
  • Court date preparation and representation
Learn More
